title: "Welcome to Estates of Pinewood"
---

Estates of Pinewood -- affectionately known as **EOP** -- is a residential community in Altamonte Springs, FL.

Our neighborhood is a friendly, gated community with tree-lined streets and well-maintained grounds. Whether you're a long-time resident or considering making EOP your home, we're glad you're here.

For HOA forms, local government links, and other helpful information, visit the [Resources](/resources/) page. If you have questions, feel free to [get in touch](/contact/).
